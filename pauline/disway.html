About

Pauline Fourest is a French illustrator and type designer. A lifelong drawer, she studied printed visual communication design at Estienne before joining EsadType and eventually TypeMedia. Her passion for outdoor activities such as climbing, skiing, and long walks in nature definitely inspired Disway.

Process

Her process included doodling and sketching to explore width, weight, and proportions, to fill the space as much as possible, while proofing through handmade self-deprecating signs. This playful application improved Disway's legibility from a distance, while preserving its fearless personality.

Specimen

Disway is a brush calligraphy-inspired typeface family, designed for low-budget, plastic-printed hiking signage. Optimized for a size of 24 pt and above, its translation contrast and flared serifs enrich the reading experience. It offers a taste of great adventures, in complete safety.
